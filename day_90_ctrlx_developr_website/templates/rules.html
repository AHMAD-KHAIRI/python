Rules

  • How to Play
  • Fair Play Guidelines

To participate in the ctrlX developR Challenge, follow these steps:


  1. Form a team of four persons, then register your team and login into the platform.

  2. Complete the tasks within the deadline for each ctrlX App challenge using the ctrlX CORE platform.

  3. Every ctrlX App challenge completed will be awarded with score points and the duration will be recorded.

  4. Complete all challenges and the team with the fastest total duration is the winner.

Make sure to adhere to the fair play guidelines.

Fair play is essential in the ctrlX developR Challenge. Participants are expected to:


  1. Respect the rules and guidelines of the challenge.

  2. Avoid plagiarism and ensure project originality.

  3. Behave ethically and professionally throughout the competition.

Unfair practices, such as cheating or violating ethical standards, will result in disqualification.
