Frequently Asked Questions

About the ctrlX developR Challenge Malaysia

The ctrlX developR Challenge Malaysia is an exciting opportunity for developers and innovators in Malaysia to showcase their skills and creativity by participating in a unique coding challenge. This challenge is inspired by the success of the first ctrlX developR Challenge organized by Bosch Rexroth in Germany.

What is the ctrlX Platform?

The ctrlX platform is a revolutionary control platform developed by Bosch Rexroth. It provides a flexible and open ecosystem for developing innovative industrial applications, making it an ideal choice for participants in the challenge.

Who Can Participate?

The ctrlX developR Challenge Malaysia is open to developers, engineers, and innovators from various backgrounds. Whether you are a student, a professional, or an enthusiast, you are welcome to participate and push the boundaries of industrial automation.

How Can I Register?

To register for the challenge, please visit our registration page and complete the registration form. Make sure to provide accurate information and agree to the Terms and Conditions.

Participant Selection and Payment

Our team will carefully review all registrations, and only those who are selected will receive a confirmation notification. To secure your participation, selected individuals will be required to complete the payment process. The payment information will be included in the confirmation notification. All payments made are non-refundable.

What Are the Categories?

Participants can choose from a range of categories that reflect different industrial sectors and applications. These categories include Simplify Engineering, Extend and Connect, Empower the User, Smarten Up Automation and Create Your Own.

What Are the Prizes?

We have exciting prizes for the winners of the ctrlX developR Challenge Malaysia. Prizes will be awarded to the most innovative and impactful projects in each category. Stay tuned for updates on prize details.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to contact our team. We are here to support you throughout the challenge.

We look forward to seeing your innovative projects and solutions at the ctrlX developR Challenge Malaysia!
