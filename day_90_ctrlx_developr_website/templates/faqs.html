Frequently Asked Questions

The ctrlX developR Challenge Malaysia is an exciting opportunity for developers and innovators in Malaysia to showcase their skills and creativity by participating in a unique coding challenge. This challenge is inspired by the success of the first ctrlX developR Challenge organized by Bosch Rexroth in Germany in 2022.

The ctrlX platform is a revolutionary control platform developed by Bosch Rexroth.
It provides a flexible and open ecosystem for developing innovative industrial applications, making it an ideal choice for participants in the challenge.

The ctrlX developR Challenge Malaysia is open to developers, engineers, and innovators from various backgrounds.
Whether you are a student, a professional, or an enthusiast, you are welcome to participate and push the boundaries of industrial automation.

To register for the challenge, please visit our registration page and complete the registration form.
Make sure to provide accurate information and agree to the Terms and Conditions.

Our team will carefully review all registrations, and only those who are selected will receive a confirmation notification.
To secure your participation, selected individuals will be required to complete the payment process.
The payment information will be included in the confirmation notification.
All payments made are final and non-refundable in case of no show.

We have exciting prizes for the winners of the ctrlX developR Challenge Malaysia.
Stay tuned for updates on prize details.
